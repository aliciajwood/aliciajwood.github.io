A Backpacker's Guide to the Timberline Trail Map

This poster-style map is a backpackers' guide to the Timberline Trail, a 41 mile trail that circumnavigates Mt. Hood. I made it as my final project for the UW-Madison's Introduction to Cartography course (GEOG 370). This was the first class I took while enrolled in their Online Advanced Capstone Certificate Program.
