United States Incarceration Rates Explorer

This application provides users with an easy-to-use interface for educating themselves on disparities in incarceration across the United States. Users can toggle between an array of incarceration-related data layers, and click on individual states to open popups filled with charts breaking down those statistics further across time, race, gender, or citizenship status. It was built with Bootstrap, ArcGIS Maps SDK for Javascript, Calcite, and Arcade when enrolled in UW-Madison's Practicum in GIS Development course (GEOG 778).
