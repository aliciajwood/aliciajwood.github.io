An American Housing Crisis Map

I made this map while enrolled in the UW-Madison's Introduction to Cartography course (GEOG 370). The lab assignment was simply to tell a compeling story with a choropleth map. This map took a stab at exploring the housing crisis that exists in america, looking at percent change in median rent in comparison to the percent change in people experiencing houselessness in the United States from 2014-2019.
