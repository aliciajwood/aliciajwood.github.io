Mt. Rainier National Park Foraging Explorer

This application is a mobile-friendly application designed to improve the visitor experience for users interested in foraging within the national park. Users can view the locations of fungi and wild berry observations in the park over the last 5 years trails, campgrounds, and points of interest, as well as add their own fungi and wild berry observations. It was built with Bootstrap and the ArcGIS Maps SDK for Javascript when enrolled in UW-Madison's Capstone in GIS Development course (GEOG 777).
